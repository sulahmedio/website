About Me

I am a forward-thinking Product Manager and Scrum Master. I also dabble in front end development. I thrive at the intersection of user experience, technology and business with a user-centric approach, while utilizing creative thinking and teamwork to develop products that will bring value to the world. I am passionate about understanding what people need and want in order to create products that will have a positive impact on their lives, and the lives of others.

My education in Computer Science gives me an edge to allow me to talk to my customers in "business talk" and my cross-functional teams in "tech talk." I am often referred to as an engaging leader with great interpersonal and finely-honed communication skills. Adept at leading high-performance teams, proactively communicating information, status, milestones, and obstacles to all key stakeholders on a regular basis.
