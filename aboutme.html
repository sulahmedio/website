About Me

I am an innovative, forward-thinking Entrepreneur, Scrum Master, Product Manager and Consultant with excellent knowledge of, and the ability to apply, the SAFe Agile Framework to existing programs, programs getting ready for a pivot, and programs that are in planning stages.

I exemplify substantial experience in coordinating internal resources and vendors for the flawless execution of projects – ensuring that all projects are delivered on-time, within scope and within budget. I am proficient at managing the entire life cycle of highly complex projects, including approval, planning, execution and closeout to ensure planned business results are achieved.

I thrive within existing project management infrastructures and processes, finding and presenting opportunities for improvement and additional structure when appropriate.

I am often referred to as an engaging leader with great interpersonal and finely-honed communication skills. Adept at leading high-performance teams, proactively communicating information, status, milestones, and obstacles to all key stakeholders on a regular basis.

Deloitte

Senior Consultant / Product Manager

Remote

For the Department of the Army, I was the Product Manager for a team that re-engineered a cloud application modernization process (CMAP) to streamline customer migration into the cloud (AWS and Azure). Increased customer cloud migration from five (5) customers per year, to ten (10) customers per month; that is a 2,439% increase over the previous incumbent.

  • Managed customer readiness to enter cloud from on-prem (start), stayed with the customer throughout the process and handed off to the operations team after Information Change Control Board (ICCB) approval (end)
  • Headed the re-engineering of a tedious process of providing customers with several documents, extensive meetings, and working sessions by giving control and time back to the customer – allowing the team to focus on bigger, and prioritized tasks; called it CMAP 2.0
  • Designed and maintained roadmap(s) for future customers to follow for migration into the cloud
  • Responsible for client relations, gathering and prioritizing customer requirements to create winning products; collaborated with other divisions to develop a sandbox environment for customers unsure of what to do in the cloud and how it will benefit the customer
  • Orchestrated a clear vision that aligned with the architects, cybersecurity, and operations teams, for both the customer and internal teams to streamline the CMAP - effectively migrating customers to cloud
  • Led the migration of a customer with a fully functioning cloud enterprise (100+ applications), for over five (5) years, into the cArmy Cloud, after previous incumbent failed to transition and communicate properly
  • Acted as a Scrum Master for multiple internal team efforts, established playbooks, roadmaps, design, requirements, and SOPs for new customers and the analysis of failed projects from the previous incumbent

  • Served as the liaison between business owners, product management team, Scrum teams, while supporting various stakeholders and end users to extract high-level business requirements to make certain features and acceptance criteria are clearly understood by scrum teams
